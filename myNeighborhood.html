My Neighborhood

Dallas is the third-largest city in Texas, after Houston and San Antonio. It is and the ninth-largest city in the United States, located in the north-central part of the state, near the junction of the Trinity River's three forks. Dallas, Tx has a lot of intresting things to do, for example you can go to Cattleack Barbeque and get some of the best BBQ you've had in your life. You can also go to a White Rock Lake and feel the breeze while you sit on the board walk and watch the ships go by, and the ducks swim. There are many things to do in this city, it just depends on what interest you.
